Time#

Generally on Sunday starting somewhere between 2:30pm and 4pm for a couple of hours. Exact time decided each Sunday on the WhatsApp group.

Location#

At the 3 sand courts at University City High School, 6949 Genesee Ave, San Diego, CA 92122.

Here is a pointer to the exact location of the courts on Google Maps

Enter the High School at the traffic light at the very bottom of the canyon, keep the left and continue ahead, the road is first going to turn right but then bring you back toward the bottom of the canyon. Continue until you see the courts, there is parking right by the courts.

Etiquette#

  • This is a fun volleyball gathering, not very competitive, unless we manage to organize some games where everybody on court is competitive.
  • Please try to serve easy balls, powerful serving kills the fun (again, unless is one of the competitive games, then full-power highly encouraged).
  • Don’t cover/take balls for a weaker player, we want everyone to have a chance to play, stay in your spot.
  • Look where you spike, hit strong only if your opponent can handle it, let’s avoid injuries.
  • The yellow Wilson balls are just for volleyball on the sand courts, no playing against the wall, no soccer, no kids (unless they are playing volleyball on the sand). They cost $80 each.
