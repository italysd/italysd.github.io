Location#

University Gardens Park, aka “Dinosaur Park” in University City Southeast, at the intersection of Governor Dr and Gullstrand St. It has a large parking lot. If that is full, park on either side of Gullstrand St. Google Maps link

Program - Sunday 17th April#

  • 11:30am: Setup at the park by the playground

  • 12pm-3pm: Lunch with Marcello Pizza Napoli or feel free to bring your own food.

  • 2pm-2:20pm: Egg Hunt for kids, please bring at least 10 eggs for each of your kids which would like to participate (filled for example with candy or small toys or stickers, no chocolate please, it would melt)

  • 2:40pm-3pm: Egg Hunt for teens, please bring at least 10 eggs for each of your older kids which would like to participate (filled for example with candy or dollar notes or age-appropriate gift)

  • 3pm-: Volleyball on the grass and soccer, don’t forget your running shoes if you’d like to play

Food#

  • Appetizer with bruschetta, caprese (fresh tomato + mozzarella) and salad.
  • All-you-can-eat pizza, Marcello will prepare and cook different kind of pizzas, that will be served by the slice. The portable woodfire oven can only cook 3 pizzas at a time, so there will be some wait time. He will give priority to the kids.
  • Pizza with Nutella as dessert
  • $15 for each person, $10 for kids aged 6-10, free 5 years old and below.
  • Drinks not included

Please signup on this Google Form, you will receive a confirmation email with the total amount and the coordinates for payment. Please finalize the payment by April 10th.

Drinks#

Maura will buy water, juice for the kids, lemonade, some soft drinks, drinking cups and ice for everybody. Please send $6 for each family via Zelle to andrea.zonca@gmail.com or Venmo to @MauraDAndrea by April 10th (please do not send Venmo to Maura’s phone number, it doesn’t work).
